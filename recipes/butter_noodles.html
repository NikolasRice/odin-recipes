Butter Noodles

Look how delicious!

This is a recipe on how to make buttered noodles, a quick and easy hot meal!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water to a rolling boil
  2. Add noodles portioned to the amoount of people you are feeding
  3. Wait about 8 minutes or until desired firmness
  4. Strain noodles in collander
  5. Add butter, salt, and pepper to taste
